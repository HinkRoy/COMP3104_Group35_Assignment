George Brown College, located in Toronto, Canada, was founded in 1967 and offers a diverse range of academic programs, including health sciences and culinary arts. Notable highlights include its strong health sciences programs, a highly regarded Culinary Arts program, active community involvement, a diverse international student population, co-op programs for practical work experience, research and innovation initiatives, unique campuses like Casa Loma and the Waterfront Campus, and a vibrant student life with various clubs and organizations.
